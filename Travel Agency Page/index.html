Discover Uttara Kannada (A district in Karnataka)

Uttara Kannada District is one of the largest districts in Karnataka with abundant natural resources and varied geographical features. It has thick evergreen forest, perennial rivers, home to some of India's finest beaches, adventure destinations and waterfalls, all of this with abundant flora and fauna which you surely enjoy.
